About Me

Hello! I'm Liuze Wu

I'm a 13-year-old developer and Yonex-sponsored tennis champion from Toronto, Canada. My passion lies in creating innovative software solutions using multiple programming languages while achieving excellence on the tennis court. As a UTR 8 player and Yonex sponsored athlete, I've won multiple national championships including the 2024 National Tennis Open A1000 Baoshan Singles Champion and A1200 Jiaxing Doubles Champion, along with gold and silver medals at the 2020 Shanghai 10th Games.

I believe in continuous learning and pushing boundaries. My diverse skill set across JavaScript, Python, Java, and C# allows me to approach problems from different perspectives and build robust, efficient solutions. Whether I'm debugging code or competing in national tournaments, I apply the same analytical mindset and determination to succeed.
